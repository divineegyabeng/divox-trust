    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: March 2026

    DivoX Trust is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

    What we collect

    When you create an account we collect your name and email address. When you run a scan we store the scan type, risk score, label, and a summary — but never the full content of what you scanned. We do not store your actual messages, links, or screenshots on our servers.

    How we use your data

    Your data is used solely to provide the DivoX Trust service — enforcing scan limits, showing your scan history, and sending email alerts for high risk detections on Pro accounts. We never sell your data to third parties.

    Third party services

    We use Supabase for authentication and database storage, Paystack for payment processing, OpenRouter for AI analysis, and Resend for email delivery. Each of these services has their own privacy policy.

    Your rights

    You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at support@divoxtrust.vercel.app.

    Contact

    For any privacy concerns, email us at support@divoxtrust.vercel.app.
