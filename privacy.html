Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

DivoX Trust ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service at divoxtrust.vercel.app.

Information we collect

  • Account information — your name and email address when you create an account
  • Scan data — the URLs, messages, and screenshots you submit for analysis, along with the AI-generated results
  • Usage data — number of scans performed and when, to enforce daily limits and display your scan history
  • Payment information — processed entirely by Paystack. We never store your card or Mobile Money details

How we use your information

  • To provide and improve the DivoX Trust scam detection service
  • To enforce daily scan limits for free accounts
  • To send email alerts when high-risk threats are detected (Pro members only)
  • To display your personal scan history within your account
  • To process your subscription via Paystack

Data storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted database provider with industry-standard encryption. Scan content is stored to power your scan history feature. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Your scan content

The links, messages, and screenshots you submit are sent to our AI provider (OpenRouter) for analysis. They are processed in real time and are not used to train AI models. We store the results in your account for your scan history, but we do not share the content of your scans with anyone.

Your rights

  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us
  • You can export your scan history as a CSV from within your account
  • You can update your account information at any time

Cookies

DivoX Trust uses browser localStorage to remember your theme preference and guest scan count. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at support@divoxtrust.vercel.app.
